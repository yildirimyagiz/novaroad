USAGE EXAMPLES

1. Quick Benchmark:
   python3 scripts/bench_report_v2.py \
     --bench benchmarks/unit_bench_working.zn \
     --runs 5 --warmup 2 --out RESULTS.md

2. Create Baseline:
   python3 scripts/bench_report_v2.py \
     --bench benchmarks/unit_bench_working.zn \
     --runs 20 --warmup 3 \
     --json BASELINE.json --out BASELINE.md

3. Regression Test (CI):
   python3 scripts/bench_report_v2.py \
     --bench benchmarks/unit_bench_working.zn \
     --runs 10 --warmup 2 \
     --baseline-json BASELINE.json \
     --max-regression-pct 3.0 \
     --fail-on-regression

4. With Plotting:
   python3 scripts/bench_report_v2.py \
     --bench benchmarks/unit_bench_working.zn \
     --runs 10 --plot PLOT.png
