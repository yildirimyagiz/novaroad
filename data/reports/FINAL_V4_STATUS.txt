USAGE
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

Local Development:
  python3 scripts/bench_report_v4.py \
    --bench benchmarks/unit_bench_working.zn \
    --runs 10 --warmup 2 \
    --baseline-dir baselines

Generate Baseline:
  python3 scripts/bench_report_v4.py \
    --bench benchmarks/unit_bench_working.zn \
    --runs 30 --warmup 5 \
    --json baselines/$(python3 scripts/detect_platform.py).json \
    --bootstrap-seed 42

CI/CD:
  • Automatic on PR (platform-aware)
  • Manual baseline refresh via Actions UI
  • Results auto-commented on PR
